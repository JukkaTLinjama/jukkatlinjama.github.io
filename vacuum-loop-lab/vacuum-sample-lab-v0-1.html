Vacuum Sample Lab v0.1

Focused lab for one morphing vacuum motor sample + synthetic air layers. Keep the tonal motor as a single morphing source to avoid beating between similar loops.

Transport

Load or record a vacuum loop sample first.

Sample input

File input is the first path. Later this can also load assets/audio/vacuum-loop.wav from GitHub Pages.

Loop recording

Mic recording needs HTTPS or localhost. In v0.1 there is no zero-crossing trim or loop crossfade yet.

Current model

      

        Load increases sample playbackRate from 1.00 to 1.10, raises motor gain up to +3 dB,
        lowers synthetic air by about -8 dB, and raises a subtle harmonic/noise layer.
        Noise layers may be summed because they are decorrelated. Similar tonal loops should not be mixed 50/50.
